Fake News Detection Dashboard - Sentiment Analysis and Power BI

  • The Fake News Intelligence Dashboard is designed to detect and analyze misleading or false information circulating online by combining sentiment analysis with interactive Power BI visualization. Using Python-based NLP techniques, the project processes news headlines and articles to identify patterns, classify them as real or fake, and extract the underlying sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral).
  • The processed insights are then integrated into Power BI dashboards, where users can interactively explore trends in fake news across time, sources, and categories. This project not only demonstrates how data analytics can be applied to tackle misinformation but also provides a real-time, user-friendly interface for decision-makers, researchers, and the public to understand and monitor the spread of fake news.
